A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the pancetta or bacon over medium heat until crispy. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta. Remove from heat and quickly toss with the egg and cheese mixture. Add reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!
