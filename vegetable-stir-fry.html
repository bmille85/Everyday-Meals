Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick and healthy stir-fry packed with fresh vegetables and a savory sauce. Perfect for a weeknight dinner!

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the red and yellow bell peppers, broccoli, snap peas, and carrot. Stir-fry for 4-5 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.
  3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, and sesame oil. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat.
  5. If you prefer a thicker sauce, mix the cornstarch with water in a small bowl and add it to the skillet. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles. Enjoy!
