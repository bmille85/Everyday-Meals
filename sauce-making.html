Tips for Mastering Sauce Making

  • Use Fresh Ingredients: Fresh herbs, spices, and vegetables make a big difference in flavor.
  • Control the Heat: Avoid high heat to prevent sauces from breaking or burning.
  • Whisk Constantly: Keep sauces smooth by whisking continuously, especially when adding liquids.
  • Season Gradually: Add salt and spices in small increments to avoid over-seasoning.
