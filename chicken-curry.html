Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and saut√© until golden brown.
  2. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken pieces to the pan and cook until they are lightly browned on all sides.
  4. Stir in the tomato puree, curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and coriander powder. Mix well to coat the chicken.
  5. Pour in the coconut milk and stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.
  6. Cover the pan and let the curry cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is tender and cooked through.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice or naan bread. Enjoy!
