Tips for Mastering Knife Sharpening

  • Sharpen Regularly: Keep your knives sharp by sharpening them regularly, depending on usage.
  • Use the Right Angle: Maintain a consistent angle (usually 15-20 degrees) while sharpening.
  • Hone Frequently: Use a honing rod to realign the blade edge between sharpenings.
  • Store Safely: Use a knife block, magnetic strip, or blade guards to protect your knives.
