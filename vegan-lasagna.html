Vegan Lasagna

A delicious plant-based lasagna made with layers of veggies, vegan cheese, and a rich tomato sauce. Perfect for a hearty and healthy meal!

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Add the zucchini, red bell pepper, and mushrooms. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Stir in the spinach and cook until wilted. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom.
  7. Place 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread half of the vegetable mixture over the noodles, followed by half of the vegan ricotta cheese and a sprinkle of vegan mozzarella.
  8. Repeat the layers: marinara sauce, noodles, vegetables, vegan ricotta, and vegan mozzarella.
  9. Top with the remaining noodles, marinara sauce, and a generous layer of vegan mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle with nutritional yeast if desired.
  10. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing. Serve and enjoy!
