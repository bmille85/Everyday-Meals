Step-by-Step: Knife Skills 101

Step 1: Choose the Right Knife

Select a sharp chef's knife for most tasks. A paring knife is ideal for smaller, detailed work.

Step 2: Hold the Knife Properly

Grip the handle firmly and place your thumb and index finger on the blade for control.

Step 3: Master Basic Cuts

Practice the chop, slice, and dice techniques on vegetables like carrots and onions.

Step 4: Keep Your Knife Sharp

Regularly sharpen your knife using a honing rod or whetstone for optimal performance.
