Beef Tacos

Crunchy tacos filled with seasoned beef, lettuce, cheese, and salsa. Perfect for a quick and delicious meal!

Ingredients

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  2. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Add the ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the taco seasoning and water. Simmer for 5 minutes until the sauce thickens.
  5. Warm the taco shells according to the package instructions.
  6. Fill each taco shell with the seasoned beef mixture.
  7. Top with shredded lettuce, cheddar cheese, diced tomatoes, and chopped cilantro.
  8. Drizzle with sour cream and salsa, and serve with lime wedges on the side.
  9. Enjoy your delicious beef tacos!
